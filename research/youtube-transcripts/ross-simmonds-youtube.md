Video Title:
Content Distribution: The Missing Link in Content Marketing | Ross Simmonds

Source:

Summary:

Ross Simmonds explains why content distribution is often more important than content creation. He discusses how marketers can maximize the reach and impact of their content by systematically distributing and repurposing it across multiple channels.

Key Takeaways:

* Great content fails without effective distribution.
* Content should be repurposed across multiple formats.
* Distribution should be planned before content is published.
* Consistent promotion increases content ROI.
* Systems outperform one-off marketing efforts.
