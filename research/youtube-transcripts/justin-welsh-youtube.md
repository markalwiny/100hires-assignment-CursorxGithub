# Justin Welsh - YouTube Research

Video Title:
The SECRET to Building Your Business on LinkedIn and Twitter | Justin Welsh

Video URL:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XAyQMUSjs2w

Source:
YouTube (Creator Science / Jay Clouse)

Summary:

Justin Welsh explains how he transitioned from executive leadership roles into building a highly profitable one-person business. The discussion focuses on audience building, content creation, LinkedIn growth, and leveraging expertise to generate revenue online.

Key Takeaways:

* Consistency is more important than chasing viral content.
* Building an audience creates long-term business leverage.
* Content should be tied directly to expertise and lived experience.
* LinkedIn can serve as a primary business development channel.
* Systems and repeatable processes outperform motivation.

Research Notes:

This video reinforces many of the themes found in Justin Welsh's LinkedIn posts, particularly around consistency, audience growth, and founder-led content. It provides additional context on how LinkedIn content can directly contribute to business growth and lead generation.

Transcript Highlights:

* Audience building compounds over time.
* Expertise should be converted into repeatable content.
* LinkedIn remains one of the strongest channels for professional creators.
* Systems create sustainable content production.

Why Relevant to B2B SaaS LinkedIn Strategy:

The video demonstrates how consistent educational content can build authority, trust, and inbound opportunities—core principles that apply directly to LinkedIn organic growth strategies for B2B SaaS companies.
