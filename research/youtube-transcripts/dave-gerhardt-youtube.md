Summary:

Dave Gerhardt discusses modern B2B marketing, emphasizing the importance of brand building, audience engagement, and creating content that resonates with real people instead of focusing solely on lead generation metrics.

Key Takeaways:

* Brand building is a long-term growth strategy.
* People trust people more than corporate messaging.
* Community creates sustainable competitive advantages.
* Content should support trust before conversion.
* Not all marketing impact can be measured immediately.
